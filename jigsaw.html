Ordering

1. Order Sources

  • Call Center Orders

  • Segregation by Location
  • Segregation by Device
Delivery
The Restaurant-to-Consumer:
The delivery segment includes the delivery of meals carried out directly by the restaurants. The order may be made via platforms (e.g. Delivery Hero, Just Eat) or directly through a restaurant website (e.g. Domino's).

Delivery market segment focuses on online delivery services that provide customers with meals from partner restaurants that do not necessarily have to offer food delivery themselves.
