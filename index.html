How Jigsaw® Sees Digital Business

We believe that every business model related to Food & Beverage in the world can be reflected into 4 aspects of the process

Ordering

Our order management system is summarized in three main sides:

1. Order Sources
• Food Aggregators • Web Ordering platforms • Mobile Ordering Platforms • Call Center Orders
2. Order Aggreggtion
• Order Digestion • Order type separation • Analysis • Order Seggregation
3. Segregation by type
• Segregation by Brand • Segregation by Location • Segregation by Device
Delivery
The Restaurant-to-Consumer:
The delivery segment includes the delivery of meals carried out directly by the restaurants. The order may be made via platforms (e.g. Delivery Hero, Just Eat) or directly through a restaurant website (e.g. Domino's).
The Platform-to-Consumer
Delivery market segment focuses on online delivery services that provide customers with meals from partner restaurants that do not necessarily have to offer food delivery themselves.
Payments
• Introduce a highly scalable, real-time, integrated control layer automating all digital payment transactions.
• Introduce a digital wallet that would carry out all customer payments
Marketplace
• To complete the whole loop for all merchants, the solution will be completed with a flexible marketplace.
• The digital marketplace will help us scale our solution and its technology to further serve market segments and adhere to customer requests.
